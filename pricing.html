Growth Care: what's included

Because "updates" can mean anything, here's the defined scope

Included at no extra charge

  • Text edits on existing pages
  • Swapping images (client provides the file)
  • Updating menus or service lists
  • Changing button labels or links
  • Updating contact details or hours
  • 2 blog posts/month with a brief provided
  • Google Business Profile updates
  • Fixing broken links and minor bugs
  • Plugin and security updates
  • Monthly uptime report

Not included: quoted separately

  • New pages (£75–£150 each)
  • New features or functionality
  • Redesigning an existing page layout
  • Logo or brand work
  • Photography or graphic design
  • Blog posts with no brief or direction
  • Domain or hosting migrations
  • Social media management
  • Ad campaigns
  • Work exceeding 1.5 hrs beyond the monthly allowance

Changes are batched and actioned once per week on a fixed day. Same-day or urgent changes are a £30 add-on unless the client is on Pro Care.
