Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated:

Who we are

This site is operated by Martin & Harding Digital ("we", "us"). If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact us at contact@martinandharding.co.uk.

What data we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the name, email address, project budget selection, and message you provide, in order to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies we use

Our cookie banner lets you control four categories of cookies:

  • Necessary — required for the site to function (e.g. remembering your cookie choice itself). These cannot be switched off.
  • Preferences — would remember choices like language or region. Not currently used on this site.
  • Statistics — Google Analytics, used to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, time on site, general location). Only loads if you opt in.
  • Marketing — would be used for advertising and retargeting pixels. Not currently used on this site.

You can change your cookie choices at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.

How long we keep data

Contact form submissions are kept only as long as needed to respond to your enquiry. Your cookie preference is stored in your browser's local storage and is not sent to our servers.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email contact@martinandharding.co.uk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated date at the top of this page.
